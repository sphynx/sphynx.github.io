Travel

I like to travel! Usually I prefer “backpacker’s style”:

  • planning my own route
  • hitch-hiking
  • contacting local people
  • trying to find a place to stay on wonderful CouchSurfing

I believe this is the best way to travel, since this way the world usually turns its better side to you, showing lots of interesting and helpful people and nice hidden places.
